This is a prototype of a top-down 2D ship-to-ship combat game. Control your battleship with WASD keys, 
aim and fire its turrets with the mouse, use the scrollwheel to zoom the camera in and out, and press TAB to take
control of another ship on your team. You can pause by pressing ESC.

The game uses a simple simulation of shell trajectories to enable ships to take cover behind islands. 
Each fired shell may fly over or impact an island depending on how far away the ship is and the distance of its aim. 
If the game detects a shell may hit the terrain before the target, a black cross will be displayed on the predicted impact area.

The objective is to sink all ships on the enemy team. Once all ships of a team are sunk, the player will be taken back to the main menu after 
a few seconds.
